# iBOM<sup>®</sup> implementation checklist

Use this checklist to plan and sequence an iBOM<sup>®</sup> rollout.

## Foundation
- Identify the highest-risk AI use cases.
- Define governance owners and escalation paths.
- Agree the “source of truth” for intent and policy versions.

## Definition and codification
- Translate intent into unambiguous rules.
- Encode constraints, contexts, and exceptions.
- Establish versioning and approvals.

## Deployment and operation
- Integrate policy bundles into runtime workflows.
- Add deterministic checks and alignment scoring.
- Monitor violations and time-to-correction.

## Assurance
- Track drift and regressions over time.
- Run periodic reviews and publish release notes.
- Test and rollback policy updates when needed.
